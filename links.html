Our Favorite Altitude Observation Points:

  • Hintertux Glacier-The Hintertux Glacier ski resort offers great skiing and fantastic views. It is the only ski resort in Austria open 365 days a year.
  • Dachstein Glacier-Starts at an altitude of almost 2700 metres, it is one of the most fascinating excursion destinations in Schladming. The glacier offers breathtaking view and many attractions.
  • Grossglockner High Alpine Road-One of the loveliest excursion and panoramic roads in the world. This road offers Majestic mountain summits, glaciers, lush valleys and woodlands.

City Day Trips:

  • Salzburg- A charming city of music and history, Salzburg enchants visitors with its fairytale-like Old Town, stunning palaces, and iconic landmarks like the Hohensalzburg Fortress and Mirabell Gardens
  • Innsbruck- Nestled in the Austrian Alps, Innsbruck is known for its winter sports, imperial history, and stunning mountain views, making it a year-round destination.
  • Hallstatt- A picturesque village on the shores of Lake Hallstatt, known for its stunning scenery, ancient salt mines, and charming alpine architecture, often called one of the most beautiful places in the world.

Our Favorite Day Trips:

  • Gollinger Waterfall- A stunning waterfall with a nice and easy walking trail.
  • Wildpark Aurach-The largest outdoor enclosure in Tyrol, home for more than 350 animals with panoramic views to the Kitzbühel Alps.
  • Lake Königssee-One of nature's true masterpieces in the heart of the Berchtesgaden National Park.
