About our trip

In 2018, my husband and I went on a two-week trip to Austria.

We landed at Munich airport, as it is close to the Austrian border and the area we wanted to focus on.
We rented a car for the two weeks so that we could reach as many different points as possible in the amazing landscapes of Austria.

Although we planned to see mostly natural landscapes, we also incorporated a few urban tourist sites.

For city trips, we spent a day in Salzburg and another day in Innsbruck.
The rest of the time we drove through amazing views to different natural sites.

We traveled through various creeks and waterfalls, climbed cable cars to snowy peaks, and incorporated one day of skiing at the Hintertux glacier.

It was a great trip, full of amazing views and activities.
